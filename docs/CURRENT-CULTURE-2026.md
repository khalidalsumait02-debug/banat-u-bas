# Kuwait Current Culture Research — mid-2026

Deep research into *current* (2025–2026) Kuwaiti social-media culture: TikTok,
Instagram, Snapchat, slang, and "if you live here you know" now-culture.
Compiled July 2026 from four parallel research sweeps. This is the content
engine for keeping بنات وبس fresh — refresh it each season.

Confidence notes are preserved where sourcing was thin. A hard **avoid list**
is at the end; it is part of the content rules, not a suggestion.

---

## 1. Platform reality (the numbers that matter)

- **Snapchat is Kuwait's platform.** ~2.43M users = **~57% of everyone 13+**,
  and it's Kuwait's most gender-balanced network — proportionally *the* girls'
  platform. Gulf users open Snap **45+ times a day** (above the US average).
  Snap fame is family-friendly diary fame: يوميات، مشتريات، طبخ. "شفتيها
  بالسناب؟" is daily speech. (DataReportal Digital 2026: Kuwait; The Meridio.)
- Instagram: ~3.0M users (70% of 13+). Formats: photo dumps, soft launch,
  GRWM, «يوم في حياتي» vlogs in Kuwaiti dialect.
- TikTok's Kuwaiti explore-tags: **#اغوى_كويتيين 🇰🇼** (Kuwait's #fyp, ironic
  self-flattery), **#الشعب_الصيني_ماله_حل** (absurdity-bait turned generic
  reach-tag), **#مالي_خلق_احط_هاشتاقات** (the anti-hashtag hashtag —
  «مالي خلق» is arguably the national mood online).

## 2. Viral moments & trends (2025–2026)

- **البيك opens in Kuwait (Jan 22, 2025)** — first branch at The Avenues;
  the queues were a national meme for weeks.
- **«لافينو» (Lavino)** — Kinder-Bueno molten lava cake from Lava Cake; THE
  Kuwaiti dessert trend of the moment, big enough to export to Riyadh.
  Successor to Dubai-chocolate; the broader wave is **pistachio-everything**
  (it caused a global pistachio shortage) and kunafa-in-everything.
- **Labubu** — per Gulf Weekly, the Gulf's Labubu craze **started in Kuwait**.
- **«كوتي كوتي»** (كوتي أمور كوتي شطور) — the ear-worm kids' song of 2025;
  spawned bunny merch and endless lip-syncs. Extremely PG.
- **Rain day = school suspended (تعليق الدراسة)** — met with student
  celebration memes every winter (e.g. Dec 11, 2025).
- **خليجي زين 26** hosted in Kuwait (Dec 2024–Jan 2025) — blue-wave crowds.
- **National Day (Feb 25–26)**: Gulf Road water-gun/foam chaos + the meme-able
  MOI water-gun ban; Hala February = the concert/shopping month.
- **«ترند الوزار»** (girls wearing the men's wizar, «خلي بالك من سروالك») —
  mega-viral but triggered a modesty debate → name-only or skip.

## 4. Slang & speech (2025–2026)

**Arabized English in live use:** مود، فايب، كيوت، توكسيك، ريد فلاق، سلاي،
كرينج، فاير، فلوب، أوفر، بلوك، ستوري/سناب.
**Kuwaiti coinages:** **دز** (send — «دزيلي سناب»)، **سحبة/سحب عليّ**
(ghosted)، **مطفي** (drained)، **مالت عليچ** (playful curse)، **اثول/ثولة**،
**چب** (+«چب يچبك»)، **يخسي**، **وناسة، شسالفة، سهالات، تهقا؟**
**Crossed-over brainrot:** **ديلولو** (covered by Kuwaiti press!)، **سكيبيدي**
(kid-coded)، **6-7/سكس سفن** (little-sibling knowledge; Dictionary.com word of
the year)، **ريز، سيقما، أورا** («خصمت أورا»)، **"you ate"/أكلتيها**.
**Group-chat culture:** it's a **قروب** not مجموعة; **فويس** culture («تدز لي
فويس»)، «يالله وينكم»، الطقطقة as the roast institution, chai-and-سوالف
imagery.
**Arabizi (7/3/5 numbers):** now **millennial-coded** — "7abibi sh5barik"
reads 2008-BBM. Gen-Z types Arabic script (with چ/گ) or English. Everyone can
still *read* it → decoding rounds work as generational comedy.
**Dialect micro-gold:** چ vs ك (سمچ، شلونچ)؛ وايد vs Saudi مرة؛ أكو/ماكو؛
**عيش = rice in Kuwait** (bread elsewhere — trap question); loanword origins:
**سويچ** (car key ← "switch")، **تجوري** (safe ← "treasury" via Hindi)،
**سيده** (straight ← Hindi)، **بيزات** (money ← paisa)، **كمبل** (blanket);
the two-letter-verb dictionary: دز، طس، جخ، خن.
**جيل الطيبين** — the boomer nostalgia self-label, now a meme genre Gen-Z
uses both sincerely and mockingly; جيل الطيبين vs جيل زد is a running format.
**Kuwait↔Saudi طقطقة** is affectionate rivalry (accents، چ، «دلوعين») — PG.
